About Bella Vista

Our Story

On Oct. 26, 2015, Bella Vita opened its first location in the Green Valley neighborhood in Henderson. The restaurant received acclaim, review awards, and the key to the city during its over a decade of serving the community.

From intimate dinners to grand celebrations, Bella Vista provides the perfect setting for life's most precious moments. Join us and become part of our continuing story of culinary excellence.
